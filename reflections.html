Lab 2 — AI Tool Experimentation

Synopsis

Lab 2 asked us to use two AI tools and compare them.

What I Learned

This lab was an interesting assingment because it allowed me to use AI models I hadn't before, like Perplexity. The other AI tool I used was Chat GPT which I had used more often. I learned that Artificial Intelligence is only as smart as you are with your instructions. In order to get the results you desire you must be specific. In regards to their responses, both models were fairly similar, and the results truly only differed with instruction.

Lab 3 — Visualization Technique

Synopsis

Lab 3 required us to think critically of the design elements implemented into graphs.

What I Learned

The graphs I chose to analyze were very visually appealing, but this lab helped me understand that a good looking graph with good information is not enough. The matter in which the data is organized will communicate the data to the viewer. Although the graphs looked nice, once you began to try to decipher the data you became overwhelmed and confused.

Lab 5 — Vibe Coding

Synopsis

Use AI to help assist your coding.

What I Learned

The improvement in my design was quite clear. I tried using different AI platforms but tried to clarify that I wanted to work on the code myself. Many of the AI tools tried to develop a css file that I could download and upload. Oftentimes these files did not entirely work, and proved to be unsuccessful. Instead, I asked the AI tools to walk me through the errors in my code, and used AI as a guide.
