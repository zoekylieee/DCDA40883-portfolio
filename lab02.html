AI Experimentation

What I Tried

ChatGPT is a Large Language Model that can only spit out information that it already knows, it is not able to create its own thoughts. I also used perplexity which was another LLM that produced similar responses but provided longer suggestions. I gave perplexity the exact same instructions in the exact sequence as I did ChatGPT. I asked ChatGPT to give me a list of groceries and it focused on making a well rounded grocery list, but I then asked it to make a list of grocery items specific to Trader Joes and to keep specific meals in mind: this led to a more detailed list that is more organized. I then asked chatgpt to make a list with those previous requirements but to minimize the items and give me a grocery list that can make multiple meals.

Looking Forward

After this excercise, I realized that there are many ways that one might use AI. There are some mundane tasks that AI may help in, such as creating a grocery list. However, AI usage becomes an issue when you are not checking the information it is giving you. In my example, Perplexity added items to my grocery list that I didn't really want there, and this is a minor suggestion, but this could lead to the spread of misinformation at a bigger scale.
